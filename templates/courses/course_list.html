Improvisation and Stand-up Comedy Courses

*** NEW courses for 2014 now scheduled, sign up now! ***

If you always wanted to try out improvision or stand-up, learn more about it, or just build some public speaking skills and self-confidence, easylaughs offers comedy improvisation and stand-up courses in English for all levels of experience.

All students are entitled to FREE entry to easylaughs shows for the duration of their course.

Feel free to check out our FAQ page. If you have any specific questions please contact us at courses@easylaughs.nl.

To reserve your slot simply click the 'reserve now' link in the course details panels below.
